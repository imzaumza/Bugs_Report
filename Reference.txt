Bugs Report

https://docs.streamlit.io/knowledge-base/tutorials/databases/private-gsheets 

This guide explains how to securely access a private Google Sheet from Streamlit Cloud. It uses the gsheetsdb library and Streamlit's secrets management

If you are fine with enabling link sharing for your Google Sheet (i.e. everyone with the link can view it), the guide Connect Streamlit to a public Google Sheet shows a simpler method of doing this.
